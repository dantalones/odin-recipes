Filipino Recipes

Awesome picture, right? That is called a Kamayan Feast, a Filipino, communal style buffet with endless kinds of delicious Filipino foods
piled onto a plate of banana leaves. Kamayan means hand and as you can tell, everything is eaten by hand.

Below are the most common / popular Filipino dishes, and I want to show you how to make them. Clink on each food to find out!

  • Lumpia (spring rolls)
  • Chicken Adobo
  • Halo-Halo
