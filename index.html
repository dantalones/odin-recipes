Filipino Recipes

Awesome picture, right? That is called a Kamayan Feast, a Filipino, communal style buffet with endless kinds of delicious Filipino foods piled onto a plate of banana leaves. Kamayan means hand and as you can tell, everything is eaten by hand.

Below are the most popular Filipino dishes. Let me give you a run down of what each dish is, as well as how to make them. Clink on each food to find out!

  • Chicken Adobo
  • Halo-Halo
